Turbulance Tycoon: Feature Proposal

Turbulence Tycoon is a Hothead Games' game where players take on the role of a commercial airline CEO, managing all aspects of their airline from hiring staff, buying and upgrading planes, and setting routes to maximize profits. I was challenged to create a feature, a login calendar screen, thinking about the whole user interface and experience.

ACE: EA Project Proposal

ACE is the UX concept to a AAA volleyball-inspired video game designed for EA Sports. It all started with the desire to create a new sports video game. After research, I discovered that volleyball is one of the most popular sports in the world. I could easily find AAA games of all other sports, except volleyball. With that in mind, I decided to approach this concept as a game created by a company well known for creating AAA sports games: EA Sports.

Infinight: BC Game Jam 2021

Infinight is the outcome game of the Canada's BC Game Jam 2021 where I participated as the only UI/UX Designer of a team of 6. Within less than 48h we needed to produce a game from scratch applying the theme "Infinite". I was responsible for designing the main menu, the game logo, as well as the HUD.

Etsy: Mobile Case Study

Observing Etsy's responsive mobile website, I identified an opportunity to improve the user journey from the home to the product's page. The current pages are too crowded and with no strong brand identity. This project has the objective of redesigning Etsy's mobile website, enhancing the user experience and making it more attractive to customers.
